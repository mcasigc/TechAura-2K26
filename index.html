About the College

Shrimati Indira Gandhi College is a self-financing Arts and Science College for women, affiliated to the Bharathidasan University. The college is located at the centre of Tiruchirappalli City. It was established in the year 1984 with the objective of providing opportunities for higher education to girls in general and to rural girls in particular. It promotes the empowerment of women through education, employment and economic Independence. It aims to contribute to national development by producing skilled and Virtuous graduates with a thirst for knowledge and excellence. It has been recognized by the University Grants Commission under section 2(f) and 12 (b) in the year 1994. The college was accredited with the prestigious "A" Grade by NAAC for the 3rd time.

About the Department

The Department of Computer Applications (AICTE Approved) aims to be a center of excellence, fostering innovation, research, and ethical values. It provides high-quality education and industry-relevant skills to empower students as globally competent professionals. Students are encouraged to engage in research, projects, and internships that address real-world challenges. The department emphasizes ethical practices, lifelong learning, and holistic development. It also promotes innovation, entrepreneurship, and community engagement for societal impact.
